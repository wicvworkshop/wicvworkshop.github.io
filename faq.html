FAQ

I don't identify as a woman. Can I still attend WiCV?

Yes, all genders are welcome to attend! Note however, all speakers and poster presenters will be women, as our goal is to promote women in computer vision.

Does attending WiCV require registering for CVPR?

Yes. WiCV is a CVPR workshop, so your registration to CVPR should include workshops.

Can I attend the poster session and the talks at the WiCV workshop?

Yes! The poster session and the talks are open to all CVPR workshop attendees.

Can I attend WiCV dinner?

Unfortunately, the dinner is by invitation only.
